Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help Studio Ghibli films enthusiasts know what movies they have not seen yet. This website will show a list of all the Studio Ghibli films. It will also show the cast of each film and will allow the feature of filter searching. I would also add a studio ghibli themed tic-tac-toe game.

Audience

Studio Ghibli seeks to help enthusiasts pick what movies to watch next and get to know the history of Studio Ghibli.
